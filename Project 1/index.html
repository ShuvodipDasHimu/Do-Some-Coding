Roronoa Zoro

Future Greatest Swordsman

Swordsman of Strawhat Pirates

Roronoa Zoro has devoted his life to becoming the world's strongest swordsman. Even before joining the crew, he was well-renowned for his outstanding abilities and accomplishments in battle. His skills drastically improved after becoming the combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates and undergoing a two-year training period under the "Strongest Swordsman in the World," Dracule Mihawk. After his crushing loss to Dracule Mihawk, he vowed never to lose again. Since joining the crew, Zoro has defeated several mighty adversaries, most of whom are fellow swordsmen.

Biographies

  • Roronoa Zoro is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates and a master of the Three-Sword Style.
  • He was the first crew member recruited by Monkey D. Luffy to join the crew.
  • Zoro wields three swords simultaneously, with one held in his mouth as part of his unique fighting style.
  • He aims to surpass Dracule Mihawk and become the world's strongest swordsman.
  • Zoro trained for two years under Dracule Mihawk during the crew's time apart, honing his skills to new heights.
  • Known for his incredible endurance, Zoro has survived life-threatening injuries multiple times.
  • His sense of direction is notoriously poor, often getting lost even in simple locations.
  • Despite his tough and serious demeanor, Zoro is fiercely loyal to his captain and crew, willing to sacrifice everything for them.
